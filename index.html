Restaurant administrator

He is the person responsible for the restaurant's accounts. He works in constant contact with accountants and bank accounts, where he is primarily responsible for managing the facility’s capital and ensuring that it is used in the best possible way.

In addition, he is usually also responsible for hiring and firing employees, payroll, daily earnings, and everything related to the profits or losses of the organization.

Chef's assistants

They are responsible for all kinds of functions important to the operation of each station, without being directly responsible for cooking or preparing any dish.

They are responsible for setting up the work area, and organizing and cleaning each preparation table based on the orders of station cooks.

Executive Chef

Executive Chef

The head chef is the third most important role in a restaurant.He is part of the administrative staff and one of the most important roles in the kitchen.

Create a menu that all diners will love: from the oldest to the youngest.
Directs all kitchen staff and their various stations.
Make managerial decisions about what is served in the restaurant.
Ensuring the efficiency of the kitchen staff.
He is in contact with all the different roles in the restaurant: from the restaurant manager to the cleaning staff.

Bar staff

Specifically dedicated to dealing with customers and preparing cocktails on the drinks menu or according to their desire. This person must have extensive knowledge of mixing and preparation techniques for various drinks.

Eman Karam

Head waiter

Head waiter of a restaurant

They are responsible for bringing the orders to the kitchen and delivering the dishes once they are ready. In small restaurants, they also clean and rearrange the table once the guests leave. In addition to other tasks such as handing the bill to diners, receiving complaints or compliments, communicating them to the kitchen staff, and sometimes even preparing invoices.

Yasser Mohamed

Delivery representative

Delivery representative

The delivery representative receives the goods and orders from the facility, obtains directions to the delivery location and customer notes, and then sets out to deliver them to the person who requested them.

Kitchen manager

Kitchen manager

Responsible for taking a complete inventory of everything in the kitchen - he must ensure that all ingredients are available.

In large restaurants, there is a kitchen manager position. In small establishments, the duties of this position are undertaken by the manager, the head chef, or are divided between both.
